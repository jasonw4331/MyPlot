Important guidelines to reporting an issue for PlotMe

Frequently asked questions(FAQ):

Q: What are the permissions?
A: Visit the Commands and Permissions page

Q: I edited my config file, why isn't MyPlot working?
A: The MyPlot config must be edited to like close to the examples found here

Q: I have edited both my PlotMe-Core and PlotMe-DefaultGenerator configs, but PlotMe still doesn't work!
A: Make sure that your plotworlds are lowercase in both configs

Q: I am experiencing normal MineCraft terrain in my plotworld
A: You are having a rare case, but we have a solution here

Q: Why is my generator not working?
A: If there is no error in the console, then it is not an issue with MyPlot.


Reporting an issue

If none of the above Q/A helped you, then you should open a support ticket.
